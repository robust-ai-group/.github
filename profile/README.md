# RobustAI Research Group

> **We study the foundations of robust machine learning.**

The **RobustAI Research Group** is located at the [Department of Informatics and Telematics](https://dit.hua.gr), [Harokopio University of Athens](https://www.hua.gr), Greece. We aim to produce robust artificial intelligence and machine learning models that can operate reliably in unknown environments.

Our group is led by [Prof. Christos Diou](https://diou.github.io) (Assistant Professor in AI) and currently consists of 2 PostDoc researchers, 1 PhD candidate, and 4 research associates.

## 🔬 Research Areas

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **OOD Generalization** | Developing methods for models that generalize to unseen data distributions |
| **Explainability & Interpretability** | Creating tools to understand the internal representations of Large Language Models |
| **Causal ML** | Understanding cause-and-effect relationships from data to build more robust models |
| **Bias & Fairness** | Ensuring AI systems operate fairly and equitably across different demographics |
| **AI in Healthcare** | Developing safe and reliable AI models for critical healthcare applications |
